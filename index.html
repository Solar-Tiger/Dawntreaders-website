Dawntreaders is a family

Here at Dawntreaders, we aren't a simple FC you join and forget where you are, forget that you exist, never feel encouraged to participate in events... NO! We are truly a warm, welcoming and inviting FC that you'll be hard pressed to feel like you weren't always a part of the group from the day it was formed!

Friends

Everyone wants friends but is always too afraid to go seeking them. Thankfully everyone at Dawntreaders is friendly is as they come from our FC leader to even the newest members!

Involvement

Between events we hold, our weekly meeting/raffle and everyone always asking if you need help with any content, never be afraid to be involved and get to know us! We don't bite... much

Activity

Raffles, weekly meetings, events... there's always someone doing something and we'd love to be a part of your activity too! Other than the down time betwnee expansion we're always doing something!

Why join Dawntreaders?

Formed back on 03/30/2015, Dawntreaders has been going strong for several years and expansions with many more to surely follow. With a good amonut of active members and leaders, it's no surprise Dawntreaders has been going long as it has! We don't just sit in the end game waiting for you to catch up: we encourage you to ask for assitance in your endeavors. We are always willing to lend a hand and help you along your journey, wherever in that journey you may be! We aren't many, but we are tight knit and can stand against even the larger FC's out there through our share love and caring outlook on members.
